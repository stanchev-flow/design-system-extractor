# Pricing — the customer platform (HubSpot)

Goal: answer "what does it cost" within two beats, then clear the objections
that follow. Audience: decision-stage buyers who arrived FOR the number.
Funnel: decision. Pricing grammar: compact opener, tiers immediately, proof to
settle nerves, FAQ for the objection tail, human path at the close. Never bury
the price; never gate it behind a form.

Voice: warm, editorial, confident, sentence case. Prices are test-instrument
copy in the brand's published shape (per-seat/per-month tiers with a free
floor), not a claim about current rate cards.

## Must-hold facts
- Tier ladder: **Free tools · Starter · Professional · Enterprise.**
- Every paid tier includes the platform core: Smart CRM, shared record, Breeze
  features by tier. Prices in USD, billed annually; monthly costs more.
- Proof: 299,000+ customers in 135+ countries.

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** Pricing
- **Headline:** Start free. Grow into the platform.
- **Subhead:** Four tiers, one platform — pick by the depth your team needs
  today.
- **Tiers (four cards, parallel anatomy — name, price, tagline, includes, action):**
  1. **Free tools** — `$0` — For getting started. Includes: Smart CRM basics ·
     forms and email · live chat · 2 seats. CTA (quiet): **Get started free**
  2. **Starter** — `$20` /seat/mo — For small teams shipping the basics well.
     Includes: everything in Free · remove HubSpot branding · simple automation ·
     email support. CTA (quiet): **Buy Starter**
  3. **Professional** — `$890` /mo — For teams running real campaigns. Includes:
     everything in Starter · Breeze agents · omni-channel campaigns · custom
     reporting · phone support. Badge: Most popular. CTA (primary):
     **Buy Professional**
  4. **Enterprise** — `$3,600` /mo — For organizations with governance needs.
     Includes: everything in Professional · sandboxes · advanced permissions ·
     custom objects · dedicated onboarding. CTA (quiet): **Talk to sales**
- **Microcopy under tiers:** Prices in USD, billed annually. Seats and
  add-ons priced separately.
- **Proof beat (between tiers and FAQ):** quote — "We started on Free and grew
  into Professional in a year. The platform grew with us; the migration was
  zero." — Founder, 40-person agency — plus the customers line.
- **FAQ (load-bearing on this page):**
  1. **Can I switch tiers later?** Yes — upgrades apply immediately, downgrades
     at renewal.
  2. **What counts as a seat?** Anyone who edits. View-only access is free on
     every tier.
  3. **Is there a contract?** Annual billing by default; monthly is available
     on Starter and Professional.
  4. **What happens to my data if I downgrade?** Nothing is deleted — features
     lock, data stays.
  5. **Do you charge for contacts?** Marketing contacts are tiered; the FAQ
     links the full breakdown.
- **Closing beat:** heading "Not sure which tier fits?", support line — talk it
  through with a human in fifteen minutes — action **Talk to sales** (primary).

## Deliberate omissions
- No capture form before the tiers — never gate the price.
- No feature-matrix wall — four cards with honest includes; depth lives on the
  full comparison page.
- One badge, one primary register in the band (Professional), exactly once.
