# Demo request — see the customer platform (HubSpot)

Goal: book a live demo of the customer platform. Audience: decision-stage
evaluators — they know HubSpot, they want to see THEIR use case running.
Funnel: decision. Form-first page: the booking form rides beside the hero;
everything below de-risks the conversation.

Voice: warm, editorial, confident, sentence case; headings may close with a
period. The demo is a conversation, not a pitch — say so.

## Must-hold facts
- The demo: **30 minutes, live, tailored to your team's setup.** No commitment.
- Run by a product specialist, on real platform workflows — marketing, sales,
  and service on one customer platform, with Breeze AI agents working alongside
  the team.
- Proof: **299,000+ customers in 135+ countries.**

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** Get a demo
- **Headline:** See your growth engine on one platform.
- **Subhead:** Thirty minutes with a product specialist. Your use case, live —
  not a slide deck.
- **Form (booking — five fields):**
  - Full name
  - Work email
  - Company
  - Company size · select: `1–25` / `26–200` / `201–2,000` / `2,000+`
  - What should we focus on? · select: `Marketing` / `Sales` / `Service` /
    `The whole platform`
  - Submit: **Book my demo**
  - Microcopy: We'll reply within one business day with times.
- **What you'll see (three parallel cells):**
  1. **Your funnel, end to end** — marketing, sales, and service working from
     one record, so handoffs stop losing context.
  2. **Breeze agents at work** — watch an agent draft, research, and route real
     work, with your team approving every step.
  3. **Reporting you can defend** — one source of numbers across the funnel,
     ready for the Monday meeting.
- **Quote (place near the form):** "The demo was our aha moment. We saw our own
  pipeline running in it, not a canned tour." — Director of Revenue Operations,
  200-person services firm
- **FAQ (one beat, before the close):**
  1. **How long is it?** Thirty minutes, plus time for your questions.
  2. **Is it a sales pitch?** It's a working session. You bring the use case;
     we build it live.
  3. **Do I need to prepare anything?** No. Knowing your current tools helps us
     tailor it.
- **Closing beat:** one line — bring your use case, leave with it running — and
  the same **Book my demo** action.

## Deliberate omissions
- No pricing tiers — the conversation owns pricing talk.
- No second form, no gated-asset cross-sell; one booking intent.
- At most the one what-you'll-see beat; the demo is the product tour.
