# Gated content — The State of Customer Growth 2026 (HubSpot)

Goal: trade the annual benchmark report for a work email. Audience: go-to-market
leaders benchmarking their team against peers — curious, not yet evaluating
software. Funnel: consideration. The page sells the REPORT, never the platform;
one trade, stated plainly, form early.

Voice: warm, editorial, confident, sentence case; headings may close with a
period. Numbers carry the persuasion. No hype adjectives.

## Must-hold facts (keep consistent everywhere)
- The report: **The State of Customer Growth 2026** — HubSpot's annual benchmark.
- Built from a survey of **1,500+ go-to-market teams** across **42 countries**.
- Free. PDF. Reading time about 25 minutes. Lands in the inbox.

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** Annual benchmark report
- **Headline:** See how the fastest teams grow in 2026.
- **Subhead:** We asked 1,500+ marketing, sales, and service teams what actually
  moved their numbers. This report is their answers.
- **Form (the trade — keep to these three fields):**
  - Full name
  - Work email · helper: "The report lands here as a PDF."
  - Company size · select: `1–25` / `26–200` / `201–2,000` / `2,000+`
  - Submit: **Get the report**
  - Consent line: We'll email you the report and occasional research from
    HubSpot. Unsubscribe anytime.
- **What's inside (three parallel preview cells):**
  1. **Where the growth came from** — the channels and plays that outperformed,
     ranked by the teams that used them.
  2. **What AI changed this year** — how 1,500+ teams put agents to work, and
     what they stopped doing by hand.
  3. **The benchmarks to beat** — pipeline, conversion, and retention medians by
     company size, so you can place your own numbers.
- **Proof line (near the form):** Trusted research: 299,000+ customers grow with
  HubSpot in 135+ countries.
- **Quote:** "We rebuilt our quarterly targets around last year's benchmarks.
  This report is the first thing my team reads each fall." — VP of Marketing,
  mid-market software company
- **Closing beat (for scrollers):** restate the trade in one line — the 2026
  benchmarks, one form, no cost — with the same **Get the report** action.

## Deliberate omissions (structure law, not shyness)
- No pricing, no plan talk — the currency here is an email address.
- No platform tour; one quiet proof line is the only brand sell.
- No second asset, no newsletter cross-sell — one page, one trade.
