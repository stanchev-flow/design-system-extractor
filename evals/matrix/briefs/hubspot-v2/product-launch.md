# Product launch — Breeze Agents, generally available (HubSpot)

Goal: announce that Breeze Agents are out of beta and convert the attention into
starts. Audience: existing customers and platform-curious teams hearing the news.
Funnel: awareness — the page argues in an arc: the reveal, what shipped, who
already believes it, then the ask. Story before conversion.

Voice: warm, editorial, confident, sentence case; headings may close with a
period. Launch-day energy through specifics, never exclamation marks.

## Must-hold facts
- The news: **Breeze Agents are generally available** — AI agents that work
  across the customer platform, alongside the team, with human approval on
  every action.
- The family at launch: agents for **prospecting, content, customer service,
  and data quality** — one per job, all on Smart CRM data.
- Beta proof: **12,000+ teams** used Breeze Agents in beta.
- Platform proof: 299,000+ customers grow with HubSpot in 135+ countries.

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** New — generally available
- **Headline:** Breeze Agents are here.
- **Subhead:** AI teammates for prospecting, content, service, and data — working
  on your CRM, waiting for your approval.
- **Hero actions:** primary **Start with Breeze** · secondary **See how it works**
- **What shipped (four parallel cells — the reveal substance):**
  1. **Prospecting Agent** — researches accounts and drafts outreach your reps
     approve, so pipeline building never idles.
  2. **Content Agent** — turns one idea into on-brand drafts across formats,
     grounded in what already ranks for you.
  3. **Customer Agent** — answers the questions your help docs already solve,
     and hands the rest to a human with context.
  4. **Data Agent** — spots duplicates, gaps, and decay in the CRM and proposes
     the fix before reporting breaks.
- **Beta stat band (belief transfer, after the reveal):**
  - `12,000+` beta teams
  - `135+` countries on the platform
  - `4` agents at launch
- **Quote:** "The agents feel like teammates who never sit in meetings. Our reps
  approve the work instead of doing the busywork." — Head of Sales, beta customer
- **Closing beat (the ask, after the story):** heading "Put an agent to work
  today.", one support line — Breeze Agents are included with the platform;
  start free and turn them on — actions **Start with Breeze** (primary) ·
  **Get a demo** (quiet).

## Deliberate omissions
- No pricing tiers — launch pages point at the next step, not the invoice.
- No registration battery; the close is a start action, not a qualifier form.
- No roadmap promises — only what ships today.
