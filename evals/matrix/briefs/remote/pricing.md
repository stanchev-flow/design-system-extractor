# Pricing — hire, pay, and manage globally (Remote)

Goal: answer "what does it cost" within two beats, then clear the objection
tail. Audience: decision-stage buyers pricing an international hire or a
contractor roster. Funnel: decision. Pricing grammar: compact opener, tiers
immediately, proof between tiers and FAQ, human path at the close. Never bury
the price.

Voice: plainspoken, systematic, operational; UPPERCASE eyebrows; sentence-case
headings; no exclamation marks. Prices are test-instrument copy in the brand's
published shape (flat per-person rates, annual discount), not a rate-card claim.

## Must-hold facts
- Product ladder: **Contractor Management · Employer of Record · Global
  Payroll.** Flat per-person pricing, no setup fees, cancel anytime.
- Every plan runs on infrastructure Remote owns in **170+ countries**; SOC 2
  Type 2 compliant, ISO 27001 certified.

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** `PRICING`
- **Headline:** Flat rates. No setup fees. 170+ countries
- **Subhead:** Pick by what you're hiring — contractors, employees abroad, or
  payroll for entities you already own.
- **Tiers (three cards, parallel anatomy — name, price, tagline, includes, action):**
  1. **Contractor Management** — `$29` /contractor/mo — Pay contractors
     compliantly in 170+ countries. Includes: localized contracts · invoicing
     and approvals · payments in 100+ currencies · misclassification
     protection. CTA (quiet): **Sign up**
  2. **Employer of Record** — `$599` /employee/mo — Hire employees where you
     have no entity. Includes: compliant employment and onboarding · statutory
     benefits administration · payroll and filings · IP protection. Badge: Most
     chosen. CTA (primary): **Get started**
  3. **Global Payroll** — `$50` /employee/mo — Run payroll in entities you own.
     Includes: gross-to-net runs · filings and payslips · one calendar across
     countries · payroll support. CTA (quiet): **Talk to us**
- **Microcopy under tiers:** Prices in USD, billed annually. Monthly billing
  costs about 20% more.
- **Proof beat (between tiers and FAQ):** quote — "We hired in four countries
  on EOR while opening our first entity, then moved to Global Payroll. Same
  platform the whole way." — Head of People, 120-person company — plus the
  infrastructure line.
- **FAQ (load-bearing on this page):**
  1. **Are there onboarding or deposit fees?** No setup fees. Some countries
     require a statutory deposit; we state it before you commit.
  2. **What happens if someone offboards mid-month?** You pay for the days
     employed, not the month.
  3. **Can I move between plans?** Yes — contractor conversions and
     EOR-to-payroll transitions are supported paths, not migrations.
  4. **What's in the price for benefits?** Administration is included; premiums
     pass through at cost, shown per country before you hire.
  5. **Do you charge FX spread on payroll?** Rates are stated on every run
     before you approve it.
- **Closing beat:** heading "Price your actual team", support line — bring
  countries and headcount; we'll quote it line by line — action
  **Talk to our team** (primary).

## Deliberate omissions
- No capture form before the tiers — never gate the price.
- No feature-matrix wall; three honest cards, depth behind the quote call.
- One badge, one primary register in the band, exactly once.
