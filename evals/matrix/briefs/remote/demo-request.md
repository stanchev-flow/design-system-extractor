# Demo request — see Remote on your org chart (Remote)

Goal: book a live platform demo. Audience: decision-stage people-ops and finance
leads with named countries to hire in. Funnel: decision. Form-first: the booking
form rides beside the hero; the rest of the page de-risks the call.

Voice: plainspoken, systematic, operational; second person; UPPERCASE eyebrows;
sentence-case headings; verb-first CTAs; no exclamation marks.

## Must-hold facts
- The demo: **30 minutes, live, on your countries and headcount.** No
  commitment.
- Run by a product specialist on the real platform — EOR, payroll, contractor
  management, and Remote Agents on live workflows.
- Proof: employment infrastructure Remote owns in **170+ countries**; SOC 2
  Type 2 compliant and ISO 27001 certified.

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** `BOOK A DEMO`
- **Headline:** See your next ten hires, run end to end
- **Subhead:** Thirty minutes with a specialist, on your countries — onboarding,
  payroll, and compliance running live.
- **Form (booking — five fields):**
  - Full name
  - Work email
  - Company
  - Company size · select: `1–10` / `11–50` / `51–200` / `201–1,000` / `1,000+`
  - What do you need? · select: `Hire employees abroad` / `Run global payroll` /
    `Manage contractors` / `All of it`
  - Submit: **Book demo**
  - Microcopy: We'll reply within one business day with times.
- **What you'll see (three parallel cells):**
  1. **A hire, end to end** — offer to onboarded in a country where you have no
     entity, with every statutory step handled.
  2. **Payroll you can check** — one run across countries, every line item
     visible before anything pays out.
  3. **Agents on your busywork** — watch a Remote Agent flag a payroll error
     and draft a localized contract, with you approving each step.
- **Quote (near the form):** "The demo answered our lawyer's questions, not just
  ours. We saw the actual employment terms for both countries on the call." —
  VP of Finance, 150-person company
- **FAQ (one beat, before the close):**
  1. **How long is it?** Thirty minutes, plus your questions.
  2. **Is it a sales pitch?** It's a working session on your countries and
     headcount.
  3. **Should finance or legal join?** If they own the questions, yes — most
     teams bring one of each.
- **Closing beat:** one line — bring your countries, leave with the plan — and
  the same **Book demo** action.

## Deliberate omissions
- No pricing tiers — the call owns pricing talk for named countries.
- One form, one intent; no gated-asset cross-sell.
- One what-you'll-see beat only; the demo is the tour.
