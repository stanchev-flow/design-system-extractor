# Product launch — Remote Agents, generally available (Remote)

Goal: announce Remote Agents out of beta and convert attention into early-access
starts. Audience: existing customers and operators who saw the Launch Day event.
Funnel: awareness — reveal first, substance second, belief third, ask last.
(Distinct instrument from the event-genlaunch registration page: that page sold
seats to the launch EVENT; this page launches the PRODUCT.)

Voice: plainspoken, systematic, operational; second person; UPPERCASE eyebrows;
sentence-case headings; no exclamation marks. Numbers persuade.

## Must-hold facts
- The news: **Remote Agents are generally available** — AI agents for payroll,
  compliance, and contracts, running on the employment infrastructure Remote
  owns in **170+ countries**.
- The law of the product: agents work inside the system of record, on live
  data, and **every action waits for human approval**.
- Beta proof: **sixty teams** ran the agents since April; the beta caught real
  misclassification and payroll errors before they cost money.
- Trust: SOC 2 Type 2 compliant, ISO 27001 certified; every step audit-logged.

## Copy block
- **Eyebrow:** `NOW GENERALLY AVAILABLE`
- **Headline:** Agents that run on real employment data
- **Subhead:** Payroll, compliance, and contracts — reviewed, flagged, and
  drafted by agents that work where the data lives. You approve every step.
- **Hero actions:** primary **Get early access** · secondary **See how it works**
- **What shipped (three parallel cells):**
  1. **Payroll Agent** — reviews every run against contracts, local tax rules,
     and the last cycle, and flags what's off before payday.
  2. **Compliance Agent** — tracks employment law in 170+ countries and tells
     you what changed, who's affected, and what to do.
  3. **Contracts Agent** — drafts localized agreements from your terms, against
     the playbooks our legal teams maintain in every market.
- **Beta stat band (after the reveal):**
  - `60` beta teams since April
  - `170+` countries of infrastructure
  - `100%` of agent actions human-approved
- **Quote:** "The Payroll Agent flagged a contractor misclassification in
  Germany that would have cost us a five-figure correction. It reads payroll
  closer than we do." — Head of People Operations, beta team
- **Closing beat (the ask):** heading "Put an agent on your payroll run",
  support line — early access opens by cohort; existing customers first — then
  a one-field capture: Work email · submit **Get early access** · consent line:
  We'll email you about Remote Agents early access. Unsubscribe anytime.

- No qualifier battery: the closing capture is email-only.
