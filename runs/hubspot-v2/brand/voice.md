# HubSpot — voice (hubspot-v2 extraction)

Derived from the captured homepage copy only (section-copy.yaml holds the
verbatim source lines).

## Register

Confident, plain-spoken growth talk. Short declarative sentences that pivot on
a contrast: *"Growing a business is hard. HubSpot makes it easier."* The brand
speaks TO operators ("your teams", "your customers", "your pipeline") and about
itself in the third person. No exclamation marks anywhere on the page; the
strongest punctuation is the orange period.

## Patterns

- **Contrast pivots**: hard → easier; "impossible growth" → "impossibly easy".
- **Verb-led headlines**: "Where go-to-market teams go to grow." /
  "Works with the tools you already use."
- **Numbers as proof, inline**: "299,000+ customers in over 135 countries",
  "2,000+ integrations", "Voted #1 in 526 G2 Reports", "resolve over 65% of
  customer inquiries".
- **Sentence-case everything** except the hero eyebrow (uppercase microlabel)
  and product names (Marketing Hub®, Breeze™ — trademark glyphs kept).
- **CTA pairing, always the same two**: "Get a demo" (primary) +
  "Get started free" (secondary). Quiet CTAs are specific: "Explore Breeze
  Agents", "See all case studies", "See all app integrations".
- **Body stays service-y and concrete**: features described as outcomes
  ("Attract and convert the right leads.", "Make it easy for customers to pay
  you.").

## Words the brand uses

grow / growth, agentic customer platform, connected, unified ("one place"),
easier, teams, customers, AI agents, Breeze, Hub. Avoids: enterprise jargon
("leverage", "synergy"), hype adjectives, first-person plural boasting.
