HubSpot

A warm, editorial SaaS marketing system on an off-white canvas (#fcfcfa — the site's own light-theme background-01 token) accented in cream (#f8f5ee) and led by a single vivid orange (#ff4800 — the brand token that fills every primary button, checkmark, underline accent, and the wordmark itself). Display headings are HubSpot Serif at weight 500, sentence case, frequently closing with an orange period accent; the hero runs an 80px serif register over a full-bleed photograph with cream ink. Body copy is HubSpot Sans at the LIGHT weight (300), 16px/28px. Buttons are soft 8px-radius rectangles with a 2px border slot at three sizes (8x16 / 12x24 / 16x40): filled orange primary (hover darkens #c93700, pressed #9f2800), white secondary outlined 2px orange whose hover washes #fcece6, an ink-outlined tertiary, and near-black neutral chrome pills only in mega-panel CTAs. Text links are INK (#1f1f1f) — often decorated with an orange underline (the site's text-link-underline token) — and card text-arrows slide 5px right on hover. Cards are white on hairline rgba(0,0,0,0.11) borders at radius 8 (product grid) or 16 (feature carousels, banner panels); media wells inside agent cards ride a warm sand (#ece6d9). Section seams and feature bands carry warm peach/pink gradient art (accent fills #fcc6b1, #fcc3dc, #ffa581). Proof rides real logo vectors, G2 badge art, tabbed case-study quotes with serif stat numerals. Two dark surfaces only: the deep-teal closing CTA band (#042729, dark-theme background-01) and the near-black footer (#1f1f1f) with cream 62%-alpha links.
