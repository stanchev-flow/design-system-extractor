## 9. FAQ — `sol-faq`

- Eyebrow: `PLANNING THE CHANGE`
- Heading: Questions teams ask before consolidating payroll

1. **Do we have to move every country at once?**
   No. The plan groups countries by readiness, risk, calendar, and contract timing. Most teams start with a clean cohort and reuse that operating pattern for the next group.
2. **Can we keep our existing HR system?**
   Yes. The goal is to consolidate the payroll operating layer, not force every people workflow into a new tool. The assessment records which systems stay and where data needs a dependable handoff.
3. **What happens to our local payroll providers?**
   The plan makes that decision country by country. It identifies which relationships should end, which obligations need a controlled transition, and where a phased handover reduces risk.
4. **Who needs to join the assessment?**
   Usually payroll, finance, people operations, and the owner of your HR data. We keep the working group small and bring in local stakeholders only where a country decision needs them.
5. **What do we receive at the end?**
   A country map, recommended operating model, migration cohorts, control owners, and the decisions required before the first consolidated payroll run.
