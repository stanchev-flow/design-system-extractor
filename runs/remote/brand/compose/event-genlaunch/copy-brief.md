## Event concept

Remote Launch Day is a free virtual launch event on Thursday, September 17, 2026,
where Remote reveals **Remote Agents** — AI agents for payroll, compliance, and
contracts that run on the employment infrastructure Remote owns in 170+ countries.
The agents are the productization of Remote's captured MCP promise ("Deploy AI
agents on real employment data"): they work inside the system of record, on live
data, with every action waiting for human approval. The page is pure lead gen —
the free Virtual pass is the primary conversion, with paid Builder and Team passes
for the hands-on MCP lab and early access. All eight sections advance one action:
save your seat.

Recurring facts (keep consistent everywhere): **Thursday, September 17, 2026 ·
16:00–18:00 UTC · Virtual · free to attend · recordings same day · paid passes
refundable until September 10, 2026.**

## Section 7 — FAQ

**Intent:** Clear the last objections standing between the reader and the form
directly below.

- **Eyebrow:** `QUESTIONS, ANSWERED`
- **Heading:** What to know before you register
- **Items (question → answer):**
  1. **Is the launch really free?** Yes. The Virtual pass is free and includes
     the live broadcast, all four sessions, and same-day recordings. Paid passes
     exist for people who want the hands-on lab.
  2. **Do I need to be a Remote customer?** No. The keynote and demos assume
     nothing. The MCP lab is easier with a Remote account — and we'll set up a
     sandbox for everyone who doesn't have one.
  3. **I can't make the time. Should I still register?** Yes — recordings go out
     to every registrant the same day, and registering puts you on the early
     access list for Remote Agents.
  4. **How technical is it?** The keynote and the payroll deep dive are for
     operators — people and finance leaders. The MCP lab is for engineers. The
     Q&A is for everyone.
  5. **What happens to my data in the demos?** Demos run on demonstration
     entities we own — not customer data. Everything the agents touch runs on
     infrastructure that's SOC 2 Type 2 compliant and ISO 27001 certified.
  6. **Can I get a refund on a paid pass?** Yes, in full, until September 10.
     After that we'll transfer it to a colleague instead — just reply to your
     confirmation email.

**Creative direction:** Disclosure accordion with exactly one item open at rest
(platform-exclusive), warm-wash hover on idle rows.
