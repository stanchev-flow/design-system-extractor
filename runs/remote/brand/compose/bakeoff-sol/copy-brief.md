# Copy brief

## Message

Workforce decisions should not begin with reconciling exports. Remote Workforce Intelligence gives People, Finance, and HR Operations leaders a shared way to examine workforce information, investigate change, and prepare a decision.

## Voice

Plainspoken, operational, and specific. Use sentence-case claims, short bodies, concrete nouns, and verb-first CTAs. Avoid hype, futurism, exclamation marks, and unsupported numbers.

## Core copy

### 1. Hero
- Eyebrow: `NEW · WORKFORCE INTELLIGENCE`
- Heading: `See your global workforce clearly`
- Body: `Turn workforce records into answers People and Finance can review together. Explore change, investigate what is driving it, and prepare decisions without rebuilding the same report.`
- Primary: `Request a demo`
- Secondary: `See how it works`

- Eyebrow: `THE REPORTING GAP`
