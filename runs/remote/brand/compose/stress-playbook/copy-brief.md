**Intent:** Clear the objections standing between the reader and the form below.

- **Eyebrow:** `BEFORE YOU DOWNLOAD`

- **Items (question → answer):**
  1. **Is it really free?** Yes. No paywall, no trial. You give us a work email;

  2. **Who writes it?** The same in-house legal, payroll, and mobility teams who
     run employment for Remote's customers in 170+ countries. Not freelancers,
     not aggregated blog posts.

  5. **What formats does it come in?** Web and PDF, chapter by chapter or the
     whole thing. Templates arrive as editable documents your team can adapt.
