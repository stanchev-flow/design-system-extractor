A light, systematic SaaS marketing system on a cool gray canvas (#eff0f0) led by a single vivid blue (#0564ff). Every text tier — hero display down to h6 — is Bossa (a geometric grotesque) at weight 400, sentence case; hierarchy comes from size, not weight. Inter carries body, UI, buttons, and the footer. All CTAs are fully-rounded pills (40px radius): blue filled primary, blue outlined secondary, near-black neutral tertiary (the nav CTA). Eyebrows are uppercase Bossa microlabels color-coded per section family (blue #0564ff / #0047bc, crimson #a52d44, gray #595b5f). Content sits in pure-white 16px-rounded cards that are FLAT at idle and gain a soft shadow + tint only on hover. Dark surfaces never appear as section bands: a deep navy (#00235c) backs media areas INSIDE white cards, and a deep maroon (#511621) inverts the active item of feature lists (soft pink wash #fceef1 on hover). The hero and closing CTA sit on an inset 12px-rounded panel painted with a pastel grey-green-blue noise-gradient art texture; text on it stays dark ink. The footer is LIGHT (#f6f7f8): a six-column 14px directory with gray links that darken + underline on hover.
